Why Kozea Community?

We think that projects become interesting when they're fun and when they're used by real users in real life.

We think that goals are strong when they're clearly announced at the beginning and when they're clearly followed when taking decisions.

We think that using our own tools on a regular basis is the only way to make them better and better.
